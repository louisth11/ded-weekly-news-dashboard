UAE Cabinet endorses Agentic AI for 50% of federal government within 2 years

⚡ High Impact Apr–May 2026

Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid formally announced agentic AI systems will run across half of all federal sectors within two years. The Ministerial Development Council was renamed the Ministerial Council for Artificial Intelligence and Development. Cabinet reviewed AI readiness across all ministries at Qasr Al Watan.

⬡ Agentic Transformation Signal

Most significant government AI mandate globally. For organisations pursuing agentic transformation in Abu Dhabi, this creates a direct regulatory tailwind — any entity demonstrating agentic AI for process redesign is now aligned with national strategy and positioned for government partnerships.
